About us

GoPlay was founded in 2008 and offers amateur and professional clubs and national federations the most complete Event Management System. This allows all those involved in the game organisation (players and staff members) to be optimally engaged in developing players, teams and coaches according to a unified and structured methodology.


  • Our goal is to support as many clubs, teams, players and coaches as possible worldwide to perform at their best.
  • A digital solution for every role and level!
  • We actively involve our users in the development of the solutions.

This way we come together to create valuable and user-friendly products.
